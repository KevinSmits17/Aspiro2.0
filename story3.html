About our brand

Niels van der Meijden, 23/03/2022

Brand vision

In a world of unrealistic expectations and high bars, we believe that everyone should be comfortable in their skins while trying to achieve their goals. Only then people can find confidence in themselves.

Brand values

Our brand represents 4 main values mentioned under here. We choose these values because they are all interconnected. Aspiring to improve your mental and physical health, but being hindered by discomfort and low self-esteem. These last two are problems that we try to convert into solutions to fulfil and improve the first two.

Our mission

Our mission is to motivate people to take matters into their own hands and get more active. Through are brand we want to give those people more confidence when they are working out. A lot of people are not secure enough about themselves with for example their body. That is where we as a brand get into action. Our aim is to let those people belief more in themselves and let them feel confident in their own skin.
