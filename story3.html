Story about Jessy O'Conner  

Jessy O'Conner, 17/07/2022
I’m Jessy, 23 years old. Last year April I found out about Aspiro Gymwear through an add that I saw on Instagram. I always felt very uncomfortable going to the gym, because of my overweight and thought people would judge me for that. After I bought the Aspiro Gymwear Hoodie I had the confidence to go to gym and start working out. In the gym I met a John who is a personal trainer. John helped me weekly with working out and lose weight. John is also part of the Aspiro Gymwear community. 
After a few months John and I became very good friends and workout partners. I think this was manly the cause of Asprio Gymwear because their apparel got use in touch and made me go to the gym. Now that a year has gone by, I have lost over 20 kgs with the help of this comfortable gymwear and the experience of John. I would really recommend using Aspiro Gymwear and joining the community, because it can also create new opportunities for you and help you get to be a better version for you.
