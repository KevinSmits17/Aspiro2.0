5 Workout Tips for Beginners

Charlie Dicks, 17/07/2022

1. Set Realistic Goals

When you start working out you want to be realistic with yourself. First look at what you are already capable of and start from there. Don’t be that persons that starts to heavy because you want to impress someone. Work for yourself and you will see results faster.

2. Be consistent

It is important that when you start working out you stay consistent. Going to the gym consistent will give you faster results and will give you a more satisfying feeling.

3. Start by training with a friend.

To keep you and friend motivating it can be useful to work out together, because when working out on your own you can feel very discomfort and can therefore be very demotivating. Next to that it will also be helpful with keeping consistent, because you will skip less of your workouts when you plan something with your friend.

4. Warm up!

What people tend to forget when they start to workout is have a good warm up. In the beginning people will not have a good form, because they are not used to the feeling of a certain exercise. This causes them to create certain injuries and that can be very demotivating. Have a good warm up before your warmup.

5. Drink enough water

Drink plenty of water before, during, and after exercise, especially if you're exercising in hot weather or for long periods of time. Dehydration will cause a decrease in performance, and extreme dehydration can be fatal.
