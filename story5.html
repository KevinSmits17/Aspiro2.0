Dave's Gains

Dave van Klinken, 19/02/2022
I was very skinny at the time, and I wanted to gain some weight and get more muscle mass. However, because I was very skinny, I was really scared to go to the gym, because I thought people would judge me for not being strong and not pulling a lot of weights. But when I found out about the Aspiro Gymwear and started wearing it, I felled immediately more confident and stronger at the same time. The hoodie of Aspiro Gymwear covered my body very well and people could not see that I was skinny, it boosted my confidence. 
 When I started working out when I was 18 years, I weighted 66 kgs when I was 183 cm tall. Now after 2 years I have gained 13 kilos in muscle mass, and I feel confident in my own skin. Aspiro Gymwear inspired me to step out of my comfort zone and work on myself. I really recommend using Aspiro Gymwear if you don’t feel comfortable about working out and are insecure about your body. The hoodie will give you a confident boost and is also designed for performance, so it is light weighted and sweatproof. Join the Aspiro community to get inspired by others and step out of your comfort zone.
